# Specialist Agent Dispatch Framework

## Purpose

This framework helps Rey decide when and how to involve specialist AI agents.

## When to Dispatch

Dispatch specialist agents when a matter involves multi-party negotiation, large financial impact, legal risk, banking terms, petroleum cargo pricing, port construction, HR restructuring, investor communication, strategy, technical analysis, or sensitive communication.

## Specialist Roles

Finance focuses on cash flow, funding, DSCR, loan structure, FX exposure, LC, working capital, and investment return.

Legal focuses on contract exposure, liability, compliance, safe wording, dispute prevention, and regulatory risk.

Operations focuses on execution feasibility, people, timeline, process, documents, and bottlenecks.

Communication focuses on tone, positioning, negotiation wording, internal/external message, and reputation.

Strategy focuses on long-term impact, partner relationship, leverage, competitive position, and scenario planning.

## Conflict Resolution

If agents disagree, state the conflict, identify assumption differences, decide which risk is most important, recommend one path, and provide fallback option.
